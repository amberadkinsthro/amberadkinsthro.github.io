Featured Publications

NBA Player Clustering Using Gaussian Mixture Models

For my Honors Thesis in Mathematical Business at Wake Forest University (completed in May 2022), I was inspired by a paper presented at the 2020 MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference by Samuel Kalman and Jonathan Bosch called “NBA Lineup Analysis on Clustered Player Tendencies: A new approach to the positions of basketball & modeling lineup efficiency of soft lineup aggregates”. My work expands on their paper by analyzing how their clustering model performs on the out of sample data (three additional seasons) and by investigating potential ways to achieve variable reduction and cluster reduction with similar results.
